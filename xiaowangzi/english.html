Group Chain-New Global Community Ecosystem

Universal Group Token injects new technology genes into the development of community organizations. To bring a more reliable and more extensive trust system to human beings, the underlying smart contract mechanism enables the situation to be automatically executed according to the contract agreed in advance, which cannot be changed or unilaterally revoked.The Universal Group Token stores historical data for each generated data block, and the information can be traced sources to prevent the transmission of false information and fraud information in the whole public chain.

Group Chain-Universal Group Token

  • The Universal Group Token aims to build the first public chain of the global community (Following referred to as Group Chain), using the decentralization, distributed ledger, and non-tamperable features of blockchain technology, to create a full-scale community ecological service platform and restruct the production and social relationships between communities. Application scenarios of Group Chain include community management, social networking, identity verification, task publishing, cross-border payment, and public property management.
  • Group Chain Integrates the underlying technologies of the blockchain, including the smart contract mechanism, Participatory Decision Making algorithm ,Peer to Peer protocol and zero-knowledge proof authentication.

Group Chain-The Universal Group Token adopts blockchain 3.0 technology architecture

  • The Universal Group Token adopts blockchain 3.0 technology architecture. Typical features of 3.0 technology include smart contracts, DAPP (distributed applications) and virtual machines. It supports multiple consensus algorithms such as POW, POS and DPOS. As for Group Chain, it provides capabilities including asset management, information storage, private socialization, credit certification and redemption.
  • Group Chain has 7-system module and the 2 public service systems. They are the zero-knowledge identity authentication system, the account management system, the information data storage system, the asset management system and the digital payment, the decentralized instant messaging tool, the underlying smart contract engine, the anonymous payment system, member token rewarding system and task cooperation system, in order to protect the quality of customer service.

Group Chain-Token

  • The Group Chain will adopt an economic model of inflation, with an annual increase of 5% of the total amount. The Group's initial token generation will generate digital tokens based on the Ethereum contract technology, and the later a digital token GTOKEN based on the DPOS consensus mechanism in the Group Chain will form in the form of mapping after technical perfection.
  • The Group Chain adopts the DPOS consensus mechanism to generate 49 nodes by voting and serve as block producers. The incentives for them are not only from the handling fee, but also from additional tokens stimulated by the super nodes . Each node will also Share an annual 5% increase in earnings. The production of 49 super nodes is generated by fair and just voting.
